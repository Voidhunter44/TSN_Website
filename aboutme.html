About Me

Hello, I’m Yousha.

I’m a 14-year-old student from Boston, originally from Pakistan. I attend John D. O’Bryant School of Mathematics and Science. I enjoy combining logic, design, and storytelling to develop projects that are both creative and practical.

My Passions

I am passionate about creating interactive experiences such as games, tools, and visual projects. I appreciate the creative process of design and the problem-solving challenges of coding. For me, the most rewarding part is turning an idea into a working project.

Some of my main interests include:

  • Low poly 3D game assets
  • Making mobile apps
  • Applying physics and real-world logic
  • Understanding how technology impacts people’s lives

My Expertise

  • Designing stylized, efficient low poly game environments and assets
  • Developing mobile applications using various programming languages
  • Writing code that automates tasks and solves real problems
  • Using tools like Blender and Canva to visualize and prototype ideas

Whether I’m creating a character rig, building a timer app, or developing a physics-based mini-game, I aim for simplicity, usefulness, and creativity.

My Story

As a child, I was curious about how things worked — often taking apart remote controls to explore their mechanics (though I usually ended up breaking them). This curiosity still drives me today, but now I channel it into building real projects using professional tools.

I am motivated by the question, “What if I try this?” and the satisfaction that comes when an idea becomes a functional app, model, or concept. I do this because I value the moment when something I built works successfully.

What Sets Me Apart

  • I pay attention to details others might overlook — like user experience, load times, and visual polish.
  • I strive to balance creativity with functionality in every project.
  • Despite my age, I’m eager to learn and master tools many don’t explore until later.
  • I am committed to continuous improvement, whether in my skills or personal routines.

What I’m Seeking

  • Gain experience through internships or volunteer work in tech, design, game development, or app creation
  • Shadow professionals in creative or technical roles
  • Collaborate on projects with other developers or designers
  • Build connections that encourage my growth and challenge my skills
  • Develop practical experience beyond tutorials and coursework
